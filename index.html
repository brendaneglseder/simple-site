Nebraska



The Nebraska Cornhuskers is a Division I football team, competing in the Big Ten West division. The team plays at Memorial Stadium, Home of the Huskers; the stadium has been sold out every game since 1962. Nebraska is one of the most well-known college football programs in NCAA history. 5 National Championships (1970, 1971, 1994, 1995, 1997) 3 Heisman Trophy Winners 46 Conference Titles 56 All-Americans Nebraska have many traditions. The Husker's defense, known as the "Blackshirts". At every home game, fans release red balloons after the Huskers first points. Nebraska has a well-established walk-on program.
